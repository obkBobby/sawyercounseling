Online couples therapy

Can online couples therapy work?

Online couples therapy can work when the room is private, both people are willing to show up, and the session has enough structure to interrupt the old cycle.

Online couples therapy removes travel time. For a busy couple, that might be two fewer drives a week and one less reason to cancel. That consistency matters because repeated patterns change through practice, not one dramatic conversation.

The basics matter. Both partners need privacy, a stable internet connection, and enough willingness to participate honestly. Online therapy is not a good fit when safety is compromised, one partner is being monitored or coerced, or the session becomes another tool for control.

When it is appropriate, online couples therapy can help partners slow down conflict, identify the cycle, and practice different conversations from the place where real life happens.
