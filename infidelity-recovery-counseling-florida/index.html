Questions

Before you book.

Do you guarantee the relationship will be saved?

No. Therapy cannot guarantee a specific outcome. The goal is to help both partners face the truth, understand the pattern, and make clearer choices.

Can infidelity counseling happen online?

Yes, for couples located in Florida when online therapy is clinically appropriate.

What if there is active abuse or coercive control?

Couples therapy is not appropriate for active domestic violence or coercive control. Safety and individual support come first.
