Questions

Before you book.

Can online couples therapy work?

Yes, when both partners have privacy, a stable connection, and willingness to slow the pattern down instead of using therapy as another place to win the argument.

What if my partner is unsure?

That is common. A free consultation can help clarify whether couples therapy is the right next step and what both partners would need for the work to be useful.

Who is couples therapy not a fit for?

Couples therapy is not appropriate for active domestic violence, coercive control, or when one partner is participating only to punish, monitor, or control the other.
