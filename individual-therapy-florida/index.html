Questions

Before you book.

Do I need to be in crisis to start therapy?

No. Many clients start because they are functioning but tired of repeating patterns that cost them peace, connection, or self-respect.

Do you accept insurance?

Accepted insurance currently includes United Healthcare and Optum. If you are out of network, you may be able to request reimbursement with a superbill.

What is the session rate?

Standard individual therapy sessions are currently $225.
