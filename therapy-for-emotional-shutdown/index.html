Emotional shutdown

Therapy for emotional shutdown when silence has become the safest move.

Shutdown does not always mean you do not care. Sometimes it means your body decided silence was safer than saying the wrong thing. The problem is what that silence starts to cost.

Who this is for

A focused place to stop circling the same pattern.

Adults and couples in Florida dealing with shutdown, avoidance, numbness, defensiveness, or disappearing during conflict.

We slow the shutdown pattern down, find what triggers it, and practice staying present in smaller, more doable steps.

Common reasons clients start here

  • Freezing or going blank during conflict
  • Avoiding emotional conversations until resentment builds
  • Being told you are distant, defensive, or unavailable
  • Wanting connection but not knowing what to say when things get intense

Questions

Before you book.

Is emotional shutdown common?

Yes. Many people shut down when emotions feel too fast, too intense, or unsafe.

Can this help couples?

Yes. Emotional shutdown often affects couples, especially when one partner pursues and the other withdraws.

Is this therapy online?

Yes. Sessions are available online for clients located in Florida.
