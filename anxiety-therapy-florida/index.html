Questions

Before you book.

Do you offer online anxiety therapy?

Yes. Online anxiety therapy is available for clients located in Florida.

Can therapy help if I am still functioning?

Yes. Functioning does not mean you are not struggling. Therapy can help before the stress becomes a crisis.

Do you use CBT?

Robert draws from CBT, solution focused therapy, family systems, attachment-informed work, IFS-informed work, and trauma-informed care when clinically appropriate.
