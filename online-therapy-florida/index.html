Questions

Before you book.

Do you offer online therapy throughout Florida?

Yes. Sawyer Counseling provides secure online therapy for clients who are physically located in Florida at the time of session.

Is online therapy a fit for couples?

For many couples, yes. Telehealth can work well when both partners have privacy, a stable connection, and willingness to participate honestly.

Is this crisis care?

No. Sawyer Counseling is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger or considering harming yourself or someone else, call 911 or 988 in the United States.
